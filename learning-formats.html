Learning Formats

Five ways to
learn with
AcademeKey.

Same programs. Same facilitators. Same standard. The format changes to fit your team - not the other way around.

Why format matters as
much as content.

The right content delivered in the wrong format creates friction. We offer five delivery modes so your team can learn in the way that works best for them - without compromising depth, quality, or impact.
Format 01

Classroom
Training

The original learning environment - for a reason. Classroom training puts participants together in a shared space, creating the conditions for real-time collaboration, live practice, and the kind of peer learning that happens when people are fully present with each other.

In-person 5-hour or 8-hour sessions Group dynamics Hands-on activities
Book Classroom Training
Format 02

Virtual
Live Sessions

Not a recording. Not a slide deck emailed over. Virtual Live Sessions are exactly what they sound like - live facilitation, real-time interaction, and active learning, delivered through a screen that doesn't reduce the quality of the experience.

Remote-friendly Live facilitator Interactive 5-hour or 8-hour sessions
Book Virtual Session
Format 03

Customized
Onsite Workshops

We come to you. Onsite workshops bring the training directly into your workplace - which means content can be tailored to your specific environment, your actual challenges, and your team's real dynamics. No decontextualized case studies. Real relevance.

At your location Fully customized Context-specific Team-wide impact
Request Onsite Workshop
Format 04

1-on-1
Coaching Sessions

Some development is too personal for a group. Our 1-on-1 coaching sessions give individuals dedicated time with a skilled facilitator to work through specific challenges, build particular skills, and develop at their own pace - with full attention, no distractions.

Individual focus Personal pace Deep skill-building Leadership development
Book Coaching Session
Format 05

Group
Sessions

Designed for teams who want to grow together. Group sessions create shared language, aligned thinking, and collective capability-building - so that when participants return to the workplace, they're working from the same foundation. Cohesion through learning.

Team cohesion Shared language Collaborative 5-hour or 8-hour sessions
Book Group Session
